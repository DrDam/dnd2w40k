    Augmentics

    His fists a blur as they smash into an oncoming hail of bullets, a man made of more metal than flesh springs over the police barricade and throws himself into the amassed ranks of arbitrators. His movement is a flash as arbitrators are sent flying, until he alone stands. Taking a deep breath, a mutant feels the automatic pistons press into his back, flushing his body with chems and stimulants to face the upcoming fight. In the pitch-black darkness of the arena, his opponent approaches him, throwing blow after blow into his body, only to reel back as his hands begin bleeding from the impact of flesh upon metal. A band of guardsmen crest over a final hill, until they spot the warboss—a ten-foot tall ork, made of more metal than green flesh, his jaw made from the remnants of a car’s bumper, and his chest fitted out to release exhaust and fumes with every rise of his chest. The warboss turns, his eyes glowing a piercing red through the night, and revs up the engines of his heart. Whatever their implants, augmenticists are united in their love for upgrading their bodies to best accommodate their desires. Whether it be to look like the biggest, baddest ganger on the hab block, or to crush enemies with pulverizing blows, cybernetics are the augmenticist’s bread and butter. Quick Build You can make an augmenticist quickly by following these suggestions. First, make Strength or Dexterity your highest ability modifier. Your next highest score should be Constitution. Second, choose the pilot background. Class Features

    As an augmenticist, you gain the following class features. Hit Points Hit Dice: 1d8 per augmenticist level Hit Points at 1st Level: 8 + your Constitution modifier Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per augmenticist level after 1st

    Starting Proficiencies You are proficient with the following items, in addition to any proficiencies provided by your race or background.

    Armor: Light armor, medium armor, shields Weapons: Simple weapons, martial weapons Tools: Mechanic’s kit Saving Throws: Strength, Dexterity Skills: Choose two from Acrobatics, Athletics, Intimidation, Insight, Lore, Perception, Piloting, Stealth, and Tech.

    Starting Equipment You start with the following items, plus anything provided by your Background.

    • (a) two shortswords or (b) two light gauntlets
    • (a) a las pistol and two power cells or (b) a pistol and two magazines
    • (a) a technologist’s pack or (b) an explorer's pack
    • (a) mesh armor or (b) a trooper armor
    • A mechanic’s kit
